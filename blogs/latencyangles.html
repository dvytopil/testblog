Angles and latency make a huge difference.

Understanding Angles

Angles might seem simple but gets rather complex quite quickly. Understanding angles can give a player a massive improvement in performance and should be considered a core mechanic. In games, more often than not, there is a line of sight from the character's head that creates what you see on your screen. We must understand that when we are in cover, our vision might be covered but not anything else.
